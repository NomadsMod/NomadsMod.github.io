NOMADS

The Nomads are a lost civilization in deep space, diversified in their respective fleets and cultures, and united together as a confederation. A mod for Supreme Commander Forged Alliance, they are added as a fully-fledged fifth faction, with unique units, strategies, and even custom taunts!
